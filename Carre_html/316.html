10     c*** This sub-routine calculates the positions of the successive points 
11     c*** on a specified level line. It stops when the line crosses a 
12     c*** structure or when it reaches the limits of the equilibrium.
13     c*** The calculated level line should not cross any of pre-defined
14     c*** limiting curves.
15     
16     *  arguments
17           INTEGER ii,jj,k,idir,nxmax,nymax,nx,ny,npnimx,plaque,
18          .     strumx,npstmx,nstruc,npstru(strumx),indstr,nbcrb,nt(2)

49     c..        (point index, curve index)

55     c.. mult1: facteur multiplicatif du segment de courbe.
56     c.. mult2: facteur multiplicatif du segment de structure.
